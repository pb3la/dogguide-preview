About

King Edward Reserve is Newcastle's premier off-leash dog park, offering stunning harbor views and a fully fenced area for worry-free play. The park is divided into two sections: a larger area for confident dogs and a smaller, quieter section for nervous pups or small breeds.

Benches line the perimeter so owners can relax while dogs play. The location is particularly popular at sunrise and sunset when the harbor views are at their best. The park is maintained by Newcastle City Council and is one of the most popular dog spots in the region.

Amenities

🌳 Fully fenced (two separate areas)
🚰 Fresh drinking water for dogs
🗑️ Bag dispensers and bins
🪑 Benches and shade
🅿️ Street parking available
🚽 Public toilets in adjacent park
🚪 Multiple entry/exit gates
🐕 Small dog dedicated area

Rules & Guidelines

  • Dogs must be under effective control at all times
  • Please clean up after your dog — bags provided
  • Aggressive dogs should be kept on leash or removed
  • Children must be supervised
  • Respect other park users
  • No food inside the fenced areas
